# Vertical-Market-Garden
Uma plataforma fixa de madeira na parede
The utilities of a vertical market-garden are important. Among them I would care to remember the question of hygiene, principally who have cat or dog. Other important factor it is that you will work vertically as well, averting so that uncomfortable posture stoop. who haven't enough space in order to plant, the vertical market-garden is a feasible solution to the problem. Who dwell a tenanted house can lead its market-garden when to remove of house.
In order to start your vertical market-garden it is necessary to have the seedling already in phase of growth:onion,parsley, lettuce, thyme, etc. Vegetables must be to planted in bottles of pops or pots of yoghourt. The choice of a quiet and cool place it us very important.
